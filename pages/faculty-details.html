Dr. Chris Joslin

School Director

Specializes in PHP, Computer Graphics, Animation, Research

bio research activities

I am currently an Associate Professor in the School of Information Technology at Carleton University, Ottawa, Canada. I teach courses provided under the Interactive Multimedia & Design program. I received my PhD from MIRALab, a world famous computer graphics laboratory in Geneva, Switzerland under the Directorship of Professor Nadia Magnenat-Thalmann. I mainly teach 2 undergraduate courses (3D Computer Graphics, and Advanced Animation & VFX) and one Graduate course (Animation Technologies) and work on research topics in the area of computer graphics and multimedia
